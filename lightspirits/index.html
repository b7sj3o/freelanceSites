About us
In 2013, we started an exciting journey into the world of show theater with "Nikalex" in Ternopil, Ukraine. For five years, our passion for fire and light performances has paved our creative path. our creative path. During our professional development, we had the opportunity to to perform on stage with famous bands such as Lordi, Poets of the Fall, Ghost, and The Rasmus. We have a lot to be proud of: five performances in one evening, 44 in a month, and an impressive 123 performances a year. In 2018, we went to London, where we started a new stage in our creative life and we are very eager to revitalize and we are very eager to revitalize our theater right here in London. With our extraordinary team of artists and incredible show quality, we are are ready to exceed your expectations and bring you an abundance of of vivid emotions.
